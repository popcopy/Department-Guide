Loans and Withdrawals

The Representative on the Loan and Withdrawal Team will be responsible for processing all basic disbursement related items, and communicating with Agents, Clients and Service Representatives on a daily basis. Representative will also be responsible for reviewing cases that have Power of Attorney / Trust / or Divorce paperwork attached to the case, and processing financial transactions accordingly.

Modified Endowment (MEC):

Distributions from MEC policies including loans and dividends would generally be taxable to the extent of any gain in the policy.  The amount of gain that would be reported to the taxing authorities is equal to the lesser of the calculated gain in the policy or the amount of the distribution. 
When processing dividend withdrawals on MEC policies, it is important that the Company have on record a Certified TIN and a signed withholding election. Otherwise, the possibility exists where a dividend withdrawal could be processed and withholding would be applied against the policy proceeds, resulting in a dividend withdrawal charged against the policy that would be greater than the amount of the check issued to the policy owner.

Dividends Declared:

Annual Dividends apportioned to a policy classified as a Modified Endowment Contract are considered a taxable event to the extent there is any gain in the policy, UNLESS THE ANNUAL DIVIDEND IS APPLIED TO PURCHASE PAID UP ADDITIONS OR WHOLE LIFE ADDITIONS, or the full Dividend is applied to pay the premium on the policy.

Trust-Owned Polices

The trustee(s) must include the title of “Trustee” with their signature(s). If a title is not included, review our records to determine whose signature is on the form. If you can determine it is a company officer or trustee, the request can be processed. One check is issued to the trust.

Multiple Owners

All owners must sign the request. One check is issued to all the owners.

Supplemental Life Insurance for Retirement Planning (SLIRP):

SLIRP is a concept that is illustrated to clients by NY Life agents to show how a life insurance policy can be used to generate a tax-deferred cash flow during retirement to supplement traditional retirement income sources.
