Loans

Whenever possible, issue automated loan checks. Manual checks should be processed only in extenuating circumstances and not as an everyday practice.

A loan form is required before a loan can be processed when:

  1. We are requested to send the loan to a temporary address.
  2. There is a change of address requested when the loan is requested.
  3. The policy is company-owned signatures of two officers, with titles, required (name of the company must appear on form near signatures) OR signatures of sole proprietor – signed as sole proprietor.
  4. The proceeds of the loan are being transferred to a bank.
  5. Power of Attorney or Custodian (Must have paperwork on file)
  6. The policy has multiple owners.
  7. The policy is owned by a trust.

    Trust Requirements

    Signature Requirements:
    • Signature of Trustees, as listed in ownership field required, must sign form as “Trustee”.
    Check payee:
    • Issue one check all Trustees listed in ownership field; the title “Trustee” must appear next to the name of the payee.
  8. The policy is assigned.

    Assignment Requirements

    Collateral assignments

    Signature Requirements:
    1. American Bankers Association (ABA) prior to 10/01/76 – signature of assignee and policy owner requires to process loan.
    2. ABA after 10/01/79 – assignee alone can request loan, signature of assignee required. br
      If the policy owner requests a loan, both the signature pf the policy owner and assignee are required on loan form to process.
    3. NYL – signature of policy owner and assignee required to process loan request.
      Check Payee:
      • ABA prior to 10/01/76 – one check payable to owner or assignee as designated on form.
      • ABA after 10/01/76 – one check payable to owner or assignee as designated on form.
      • NYL – One check payable to owner and assignee unless otherwise designated on form.

    Absolute Assignments:

    Signature Requirements:
    • Assignee only
    Check payee:
    • ABS - Assignee only

Requirements

  1. Always view current policy information.
  2. Check premium paid-to date on whole life policies; the premium must be paid within the grace
      If the premium is more than 31 days past due, and does not have the APL provision, pay the premium by loan then, the next day, send out the remaining loan balance. If the policy has the APL provision, hold back enough to cover the premium(s) due and issue the loan for the amount available less the premium(s) due or the requested amount, if less.
  3. A policy on a NYL-A-Plan mode can have a loan taken if outside the grace period, but the loan check must be manual and the value of the premium to bring within the grace period will be withheld.
  4. If the current year’s dividend is on the record and premiums are paid to date but not paid to the anniversary, deduct the current dividend.
  5. Review for a Change Pending Indicator (CPI) present on record to determine if any other actions are required.

To Process an automated cash loan:

From COMMON MONITOR go to the LOAN header
Fill in the following information

  1. Policy Number
  2. Amount Requested
  3. Sur Name
  4. Tax withholding election
  5. Check Type
    • B - Signed loan form
    • L - No loan form received

Requirements to process Cash Loan via EFT:

Client must have Check-O-Matic information on record; be sure to verify that the owner is also the account holder. Review the APD and PRD screens for verification.

  • EFT DISB OPT "Y" if chosen.

To process an Automated Cash Loan Check via SSF:

You can process an automated cash loan through SSF, 3 different path ways:

  1. LVQ button
  2. View > Quotes > Loan Value
  3. Process > Process Loan

From COMMON MONITOR go to TERM header and then to LOAN sceen.
Fill in the following information

  1. Policy Number
  2. Sur Name
  3. TRANS CODE "MH"
  4. LOAN AMT (Amount Requested)
  5. Tax withholding election
  6. LOAN APPLIED TO "B0000000000" OR "BLCHECK000"
  7. Check Type
    • B000000000 - Signed loan form
    • BLCHECK000 - No loan form received
  8. OFFSET "C $.$$"

To process a Manual Cash Loan:

A loan must be processed manually when

  1. There is an assignment on the policy.
  2. There is termination data disbursement on the policy.
  3. Premiums are not paid to date (hold back premiums due from the loan value).
  4. When the check is being sent via overnight mail.

From COMMON MONITOR go to TERM header and then to LOAN sceen.
Fill in the following information

  1. Policy Number
  2. Sur Name
  3. TRANS CODE "MH"
  4. LOAN AMT (Amount Requested)
  5. Tax withholding election
  6. LOAN APPLIED TO "B0000000000" OR "BLCHECK000"
  7. Check Type
    • B000000000 - Signed loan form
    • BLCHECK000 - No loan form received
  8. OFFSET "C $.$$"
  9. Press F5


On the second screen of the TREM transaction verify name and address of payee.


If the name and address of the payee is missing or different, put a “X” in the NAME and ADDRESS fields on the first line. Type the new name and address information in NAME, ADDRESS and ZIP CODE fields on the right side of the screen.

Under REASON CODE Type
  1. Cash loan
  2. B or L
  3. Initials
  4. Press F5 tp process
Be sure to prepare your check folder with the following:
  • A print out of the current Loan Value Quote MRB
  • Completed overnight mailing envelope.

Deleting/Modifying Automated Cash Loans:

Note: Automated cash loans processed through Common Monitor or SSF are all modified/deleted through Common Monitor in Rumba.

  1. Log in to Common Monitor.
  2. Type ROAN and hit Enter. (R is for Recall and is put in place of the first letter of the header used for a transaction, e.g., LOAN is ROAN, WDIV would be RDIV, etc.)
  3. Type in the policy number in the policy number field and hit Enter.
  4. Confirm the correct transaction displays
  5. To delete the transaction, type “delete” or “del” at the top of the screen and hit Enter.
  6. To modify the transaction, take the following steps:
      Type “modify” or “mod” at the top of the screen and hit Enter.
      Make the necessary changes to the transaction and hit Enter.
      Type “accept” or “acc” at the top of the screen and hit F5 to complete the transaction.

Deleting Manual Check in CABS :

Note: Can only be deleted on the date the transaction was input.

  1. Log in to CABS.
  2. Type 10 in the SELECTION field on the main menu and hit Enter.
  3. Type 14 on the next screen and hit Enter.
  4. On the next screen, in the REFERENCE TYPE/NUMBER field, enter p/policy number and hit Enter.
  5. On the next screen, find the pending loan transaction (based on account date and audit code) and input a ‘D’ in the SEL field preceding the transaction to be deleted.
  6. Hit Enter.
  7. Type ‘Y’ in the prompt at the bottom of the screen to delete the transaction (typing ‘N’ will bypass delete) and hit Enter. (When the transaction disappears, and you are back at the common inquiries menu (10), your transaction has been deleted).
